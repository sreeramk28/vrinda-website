    ABOUT US

    We know the art of recruitment







    Incorporated in 2006, this entrepreneurial journey has metamorphosed to a transnational organisation with business operations in Asia, Europe & US.

    With a meticulous focus on driving successful hires, we’re a new type of partner and a new type of recruiting system. Our systematic, process driven approach to recruitment ensures engagement with the right talent and a superior candidate experience.

    Our offices in London, San Jose, Dubai, New Delhi & Gurgaon are independent units lead by our regional directors, supported by highly talented back-office in Karnal (Haryana, India).

    Our clients include 32 of fortune 100 companies.
